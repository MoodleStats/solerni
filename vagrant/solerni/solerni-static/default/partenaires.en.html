Partners

Orange

Orange is one of the main telecommunications operators in the world, with a turnover of 43.5 billion Euros in 2012 and 166,000 employees on 30th September 2013, including 102,000 in France. With business in 32 countries, the Orange Group serves over 232 million customers, including 175 million mobile customers and 15 million high speed fixed line customers worldwide. Orange is also one of the world leaders in telecommunications services for multinational companies under the Orange Business Services brand. Orange is listed on the NYSE Euronext Paris (ORA symbol) and on the New York Stock Exchange (ORAN symbol).
